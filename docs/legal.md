## What Torfin is

Torfin is open-source client software (MIT license) that helps you:

- Browse **public metadata** (titles, posters, descriptions) from catalog APIs such as Cinemeta
- Query **Stremio-compatible addons** that you choose and configure
- Resolve torrent references through **your own** [Torbox](https://torbox.app) account
- Download or import files into **your own** Jellyfin library or storage you control

Torfin does **not** host, store, index, or distribute movies, TV shows, or other copyrighted works. It does not operate Torrentio, Torbox, Cinemeta, or any third-party addon.
